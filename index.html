About Me

 My name is Zachariah Dellimore, I am a African American software developer based in Richmond Virginia I am currently a sophmore at Virginia Commonwealth University where I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Math with a GPA of 3.6. I went to high school at Western Albemarle High School and graduated with an advanced degree, magna cum laude, and with a GPA of 3.6.

 During high school I was a part of my schools robotics team. I started as a programmer for my team Cable Management but after my first year I became the leader of the team and had to manage building, programming, and teaching. Teaching my team members how to code was very impactful on me because it increased my love of coding as well as my understanding of code. The leadership experience I gained from that experience has stuck with me and has allowed me to improve my group projects' efficiency and quality.

 Outside of school I prefer to spend my time coding projects that interest me. Some projects that I have made are a Unix Shell program made in the C programming language, an email client for Linix Systems made in the C programming language, and most recently, a fully customizable Minesweeper game made in C++ using the SFML game development library.
